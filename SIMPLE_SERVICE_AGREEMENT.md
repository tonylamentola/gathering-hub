# Simple Service Agreement

## The Parties

This agreement is between:

- Provider: Anthony Lamentola
- Client: The Gathering Hub

## Service

Provider will supply a lightweight managed website service for The Gathering Hub, including:

- website hosting/setup as agreed
- routine content updates
- menu, upcoming events, quick updates, blog, and photo support
- access to a simple content portal when appropriate

## Monthly Rate

- `$50/month`

This monthly rate is intended to cover the basic website service and light support described above.

## What Is Included

- normal content edits
- menu updates
- upcoming event/flyer updates
- homepage quick updates
- light blog/update assistance
- basic support for keeping the site current

## What Is Not Included

Unless separately agreed, the monthly service does not include:

- major redesigns
- custom app features
- large migrations
- branding work
- unlimited copywriting
- complex integrations

## Hosting / Domain

The parties will confirm:

- who owns the domain
- who pays for hosting
- whether the client already has an existing website/domain to connect

## Content Responsibility

- Client is responsible for providing accurate business information, approved photos, and approved updates.
- Provider is not responsible for incorrect facts supplied by the client.

## Photo / Content Rights

- Client confirms they have the right to use any photos, flyers, logos, menus, or content they provide.

## Update Requests

- Client may send update requests through the agreed communication channel.
- Provider may review and confirm requests before publishing changes.

## Payment

- Monthly payment is due through the Stripe billing link or other agreed method.
- Service may be paused if payment is not kept current.

## Cancellation

- Either side may end the monthly service with reasonable notice.
- Any prepaid period already paid for remains covered through that billing period unless otherwise agreed.

## Simple Intent

This is meant to be a straightforward, friendly working agreement so both sides are clear on the monthly website support arrangement.
